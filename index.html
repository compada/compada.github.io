Mission

At Compada we are on a mission to help start-ups, small businesses, private equity firms, and consultants access a leading advisory network in order to find immediate knowledge and solutions to problems impacting their operations. Unlike the traditional method of reaching experts through who you or your connections know, Compada focuses on advisors with extensive experience in their respective fields who have already indicated their willingness and availability to collaborate. Compada is not a recruiting platform, but an advisory and knowledge network platform that will help you find the right expert to help you move ahead. At Compada, everyone is part of your company!

Investors

We view our relationships with investors as long-term partnerships, not deals. We look to our investors for guidance around best practices learned over past investments and careers to help navigate unforeseen challenges and improve our collective success. Having served in big and small companies, our team recognizes the power of knowledge and business experience. We also recognize that any entrepreneur venture is ridden with uncertainty and risk, which is why we believe in the collective partnership.

If you are an investor in early stage, pre-seed, seed, and/or series A companies and would like to get involved in improving prosperity, and sustainability across the knowledge networks and management consulting industry we would love to meet you.
